NCI-supported clinical trials are those sponsored or otherwise financially supported by NCI. See our guide, Steps to Find a Clinical Trial, to learn about options for finding trials not included in NCI's collection.

Search Tip: Skip any items that are unknown or not applicable or try our basic search.

CANCER TYPE / CONDITION ?
You must first select a cancer type or condition. Based on that selection, you will then be able to choose additional options, if applicable, from the subsequent fields.
AGE ?
Enter the age of the participant
KEYWORDS / PHRASES ?
Search by word or phrase (use quotation marks with phrases)
LOCATION ? Use these options to search for trials near a specific zip code; in a country, city, and state; at a particular hospital/institution; or at the National Institutes of Health clinical center. The default selection will search for trials in all available locations.
TRIAL TYPE ?
Select the type of trial for your search. You may check more than one box or select "All."
DRUG / TREATMENT ?
Search for a specific drug or intervention. You can use the drug's generic or brand name.
TRIAL PHASE ?
Search by one or more trial phases
TRIAL ID ?
Separate multiple IDs with commas or semicolons
TRIAL INVESTIGATORS ?
Search by Trial Investigators
LEAD ORGANIZATION ?
Search by Lead Organization
